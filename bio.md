Uli Hitzel is a hands-on AI technologist and ecosystem builder working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, business transformation, and human creativity. He helps enterprises and startups build with AI rather than just buy it, guiding teams through practical implementation while exploring how AI augments human capabilities across industries.

With over 25 years of experience bridging emerging technologies and practical business applications, Uli currently leads naida, a consultancy driving AI adoption across Southeast Asia, and founded Electric Minds, a non-profit initiative tackling societal challenges through collaborative AI ecosystems. His recent roles include interim GenAI Lab Lead at The HEINEKEN Company and Digital Technologist at Dyson, where he led digital transformation and AI-powered manufacturing innovation.

Based in Singapore for over 14 years, Uli has been a Fellow at the National University of Singapore since 2020, teaching AI and DevOps courses that shape the next generation of technology professionals. His work spans healthcare (critical digital infrastructure for Raffles Medical Group's COVID-19 response), manufacturing, finance, and public sector transformation, building on earlier experience at Microsoft, IBM, Red Hat, and Yahoo.

Author of books including "Neurons & Nonsense: How AI Rewired My Creative DNA" and "The LLM Stack," Uli writes, teaches, and mentors extensively. He's passionate about making AI accessible and practical while preserving the human elements that matter most in our increasingly digital world.
