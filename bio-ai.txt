Uli Hitzel is a technologist, author, innovator, and visionary in the field of artificial intelligence and creativity. With a background in both technology and humanities, he possesses a unique ability to bridge the gap between these seemingly disparate disciplines. Throughout the years, Uli has been interested in the possibilities that emerge when human ingenuity meets machine intelligence, This interest has resulted in published books, speaking engagements, workshops, and facilitating conversations to drive change across various media formats. His curiosity and enthusiasm for collaboration have gained appreciation from industry experts and readers alike. In his latest book, Uli shares his personal experiences with AI, inviting readers to explore the transformative potential of human-machine collaboration for creativity and innovation.
